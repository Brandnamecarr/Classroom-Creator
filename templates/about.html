About page


Classroom Creator is an application that helps convert your classroom data to Google Classroom, saving you countless hours of initalization time.


The premise of Classroom Creator is simple: taking student data from Canvas (or a CSV file) and initializing your new Google Classroom.


Let Classroom Creator handle the formatting and tedious data entry on your behalf.
